## Who We Are — A Product Company at Heart

iTrend Solution is a product-based technology company that has built, manufactured, and
delivered high-quality products and commerce technology to customers around the world since 2016.

We build products, platforms, and brand ecosystems that scale across six global marketplaces
on three continents — powered by our Chennai Head Office, an India-based R&D hub, and a China
operations base. Every capability a future-ready commerce business needs lives under one
integrated roof: Software Development, Research & Development, Product Sourcing, Cataloguing,
Content Creation, Design, Digital Marketing, Supply Chain Management, and Logistics Operations.

**What makes us different:**

- **End-to-End Ownership** — Products, listings, advertising, and fulfilment run through one
  fully integrated supply chain, not fragmented vendors.
- **Proven Track Record** — 9+ years, 5,000+ SKUs, and $25M+ in marketplace sales across
  six global storefronts.
